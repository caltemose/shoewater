Destiny Resources

This page contains helpful online resources for Destiny for LFG, stats and gear management.

LFG

  • Main Destiny Site
  • www.destinylfg.net
  • www.the100.io

Gear/Inventory

  • destinyitemmanager.com - Manage your inventory
  • light.gg - Tons of useful inventory stuff
  • destinysets.com - Track gear you don’t have

Stats

  • destinytracker.com - Check player stats
  • raid.report - Check a player’s raid stats/history
  • destinytrialsreport.com - Check out your trials opponents
